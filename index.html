The Soup

Soup was invented in the early 1897's and has been one of the most famous meals today. Soup has brought happiness and joy to everyone who has tried it.

A warm bowl of soup can hit the spot on a cold day. It can also be a healthy way to start off a meal because it tends to be high in vegetables, filling and low in energy density. Homemade soup is a better choice than canned soup, however, because canned soup often contains the chemical BPA and is high in sodium.

Is soup good?

Watery foods, such as soups, can help you lose weight because of their low calorie density, or relatively low number of calories compared to their serving size. ... Fiber and protein are filling nutrients, so choose a soup with ingredients such as vegetables, beans and lean protein.

This means that you can eat a lot of soup without consuming that many calories, and the soup will help fill you up so you don't eat as much of other, higher-calorie foods. A healthy rate of weight loss is 1 to 2 pounds per week, so losing 20 pounds will take at least 10 to 20 weeks.
